ReConsider Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

Data Collection

ReConsider is committed to protecting your privacy. The extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

ReConsider stores the following data locally in your browser:

  • Your list of websites to monitor
  • Your difficulty settings
  • Any custom phrases you've added

This data never leaves your browser and is only used to provide the extension's core functionality.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your settings locally
  • Web Navigation: To detect when you visit monitored websites
  • Host permissions: To show the overlay on monitored websites

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me through the feedback form in the extension.
